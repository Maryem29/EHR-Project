About MedCloud

Welcome to MedCloud, your one and only secure electronic health record system with functional and helpful features. Founded by our lovely and optimistic team in 2025. This platform is built with the sole purpose of aiding doctors with medical record-keeping and organising patient files for less work and clutter and better efficiency! We ensure seamless record organisation so you can ensure better patient care. MedCloud flair for better care ◡̈!

Our Mission

This startup’s top priority is empowering healthcare professionals with a user-friendly, secure, and efficient EHR system for their daily use. What makes us different? The healthcare providers’ opinion and experience matters to us! Our system is designed with multiple feedback from healthcare professionals from across the globe. We centered our design, project outlook, and system usability and functionality around our core users. We can guarantee easy use and great functions for your healthcare providers.
